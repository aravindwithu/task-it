How Task It works ?

Register and create your account.

Construct and enhance your profile.

Link friends, family, collegue, employee, and etc accounts.

You can initiate or accept a task to or from your friend.

If you Intiate the task, then your friend should accept the task.

If you recived the task you can accept the same and start to work on it.

You can add relevent documents to the task.

You can give regular update on progress of the task.

Recive regular emails for requests and updates of the task.
